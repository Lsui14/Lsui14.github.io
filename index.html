Echoes of Xyphoria

3ds Max | Substance Painter | PhotoShop | Unity | Audacity

Echoes of Xyphoria is my most ambitious project to date: a space-themed roguelike that combines fast-paced action, strategic progression, and an immersive narrative. As the game designer, I led the creation of the entire Game Design Document (GDD), designing mechanics, dynamics, perks, characters, the storyline, and the overall game flow. I also designed all in-game interfaces, ensuring a cohesive and intuitive user experience, and personally created, edited, and programmed the sound effects to enhance immersion. Developed in Unity and programmed in C#, Echoes of Xyphoria showcases my ability to craft compelling systems, balance progression, and build a rich, engaging universe. The game is available on itch.io, where players can experience its challenging gameplay and carefully designed aesthetics.
